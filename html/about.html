About Green Hydrogen PtX Ethiopia

Green Hydrogen PtX Ethiopia is an initiative dedicated to advancing sustainable energy solutions through green hydrogen and Power-to-X technologies. Our mission is to support Ethiopia's transition to a clean energy economy, fostering innovation, investment, and collaboration in the renewable energy sector.

Our Vision

To establish Ethiopia as a leading hub for green hydrogen and PtX technologies, driving economic growth and environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To facilitate knowledge-sharing, policy development, and investment in clean hydrogen technology for a greener future.

Our Partners

We collaborate with key stakeholders, including the Ministry of Water & Energy, GIZ Ethiopia, Ethiopian Airlines, and global clean energy organizations.
